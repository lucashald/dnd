Ingrid Runestone

About Ingrid

Ingrid Runestone is the half-dwarf proprietor and manager of the Glacier's Rest Inn, operating it alongside her father Ingvar. While she appears to be a straightforward innkeeper, she serves as a valuable Harper affiliate and intelligence contact, working closely with Skylark. Rather than undertaking field missions, Ingrid specializes in information gathering, message passing, and serving as a nexus for Harper communications across the region.

Appearance & Background

Ingrid stands at a height between her parents—taller than a typical dwarf but shorter than a full human. She bears her dwarven father's sturdy frame and practical nature, while her human mother's heritage manifests in her facial features and longer stature. She maintains a warm, professional demeanor that makes visitors feel at home and loosens tongues over ale and hearth fires.

Family

Ingvar Runestone - Father

Race: Dwarf (Mountain Dwarf)

Role: Owner and keeper of the Glacier's Rest Inn

Ingvar is a no-nonsense dwarf who has run the inn for many years. He is practical, reliable, and provides a stable home base for Harper operations in Fjord's End. While not an active agent, Ingvar is aware of and supports his daughter's Harper affiliations. He is known for his excellent ale selection and fair dealing with travelers.

Ingrid's Mother

Name: Mira Runestone

Race: Human

Affiliation: The Harpers (active agent)

Mira is currently on an extended Harper mission away from Fjord's End. Her whereabouts are not common knowledge, though Ingrid maintains contact through Harper channels. Mira is a skilled operative who instilled in her daughter the values of the Harpers—discretion, service, and the importance of information networks. She is the source of Ingrid's Harper connections and training.

Role as Harper Affiliate

Ingrid operates as an information nexus and message relay for Harper operations in the northern reaches. Her position as innkeeper provides perfect cover for gathering intelligence, as travelers naturally share stories and information over meals and drinks. She maintains secure channels to key Harper contacts and can facilitate communication between agents in the field.

Specialization: Information gathering, secure message passing, contact facilitation

Field Operations: Rarely undertakes field missions; primarily operates as a stationary asset and communication hub

Harper Network Contacts

Primary Contact

Skylark: Regional Harper agent and scout. Skylark is Ingrid's primary point of contact and collaborator. Messages between Skylark and other Harper agents are often routed through Ingrid at the inn.

Regional Contacts

Uthmere Contact - Aldric Thorne: A retired merchant and Harper affiliate operating in the town of Uthmere. Aldric runs a small curio shop and serves as a safe house for traveling agents. He can provide supplies, information about regional trade and politics, and can relay messages between Fjord's End and other northern settlements.

Ilmwatch Contact - Captain Elara Voss: A skilled scout and military liaison in the settlement of Ilmwatch. Captain Voss maintains awareness of military movements and threats in the region. She has reliable access to regional correspondence and can provide intelligence on threats approaching from the south or east.

The Glacier's Rest Inn

The inn serves as both a genuine business and a Harper safe house. It offers:

  • Best Room in the House: A corner chamber overlooking the harbor, often reserved for traveling agents or VIP guests
  • Secure Lodging: Safe rooms for those requiring discretion
  • Information Hub: A gathering place where travelers share news and gossip
  • Message Station: Reliable message relay point for Harper communications
  • Excellent Provisions: Quality food and drink that encourage patrons to linger and talk

Working with Ingrid

Ingrid is discreet, professional, and reliable. Those seeking Harper contacts or requiring secure communication channels should approach her with appropriate discretion. She will not openly acknowledge her Harper affiliations to casual patrons, but those sent by Skylark or introduced through proper channels will find her a valuable ally.

She prefers to conduct sensitive business in private rather than at the bar, and often takes a personal interest in the well-being of those she helps. Her information network is extensive but carefully maintained—she reveals only what is necessary and trusts sparingly.
