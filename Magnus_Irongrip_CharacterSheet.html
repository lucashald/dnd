Notes & Backstory Summary

Origin: Last survivor of the Irongrip clan of Earthfast, a dwarven city destroyed by the black dragon Putrifax 5 years ago.

Current Role: Captain of the merchant ship "The Silt Runner" and active Harper agent monitoring the northern fjord regions.

Heritage: Descendant of the dwarf who slew the white dragon of Giantspire Peak 700 years ago with the legendary spear Snowpiercer. His family established mines in Giantspire before losing them to orcs 500 years ago.

Motivations: Preserve Earthfast's memory through song and story, protect others from similar tragedies, eventually seek vengeance against Putrifax, and possibly reclaim his family's ancestral mine in Giantspire Peak.

Connections: Works closely with Skylark (Harper ranger). Known and trusted in Fjord's End. Has contacts throughout the Harper network and among Earthfast refugees.
