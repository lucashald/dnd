Class Features (Ranger - Hunter)

Spellcasting: Wisdom-based spellcasting (Spell Save DC 12, Spell Attack +4)

Favored Enemy: Red Wizards and Thayan agents (humanoids). Advantage on Survival checks to track them and Intelligence checks to recall information about them. Learn one language associated with them (Thayan).

Natural Explorer (Arctic):

  • Difficult terrain doesn't slow group's travel in arctic regions
  • Group can't become lost except by magical means
  • Alert to danger even while engaged in another activity (foraging, navigating, tracking)
  • Move stealthily at normal pace when traveling alone
  • Find twice as much food and water when foraging
  • Learn exact number, sizes, and how long ago creatures passed through when tracking

Fighting Style - Archery: +2 bonus to attack rolls with ranged weapons (included in stats below)

Primeval Awareness: Action and 1 spell slot to sense types of creatures (aberrations, celestials, dragons, elementals, fey, fiends, undead) within 1 mile (6 miles in favored terrain). No information on location or number.

Hunter's Prey - Colossus Slayer: Once per turn, when you hit a creature with a weapon attack and it's below max HP, deal an extra 1d8 damage.

As Skylark's companion, Adam:

  • Can take Attack, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, or Help actions on command
  • Is exceptionally intelligent for a raven
  • Serves as scout, messenger, and lookout
  • Often watches the pier and alerts Skylark to arrivals

Personality & Traits

Personality Traits:

  • I watch and listen carefully before speaking or acting
  • I often consult with Adam before making decisions

Ideals:

  • Protection: Those who cannot defend themselves deserve defenders (Good)
  • Knowledge: Understanding threats is the first step to defeating them (Neutral)

Bonds:

  • The Harpers gave me purpose and direction; I serve them faithfully
  • Fjord's End is under my protection
  • Adam is my closest companion and friend

Flaws:

  • I trust my instincts and observations over others' words
  • I can be overly cautious about sharing information

Harper Mission & Activities

Primary Mission: Monitor threats in the northern territories, particularly Red Wizard activities, supernatural dangers, and political instability.

Current Objectives:

  • Establish Fjord's End as a Harper safe haven
  • Track Red Wizard movements near the Stone Forest
  • Investigate ancient magical sites (Cursed Pool, Giantspire Peak)
  • Build network of reliable contacts and informants
  • Identify potential Harper recruits
  • Protect remote settlements from various threats

Key Contacts:

  • Magnus Irongrip: Fellow Harper, trusted ally, handles sea-based operations
  • Regional Harper Network: Agents throughout northern territories
  • Local Allies: Various contacts in Fjord's End and nearby settlements (most unaware of Harper connections)

Combat Tactics

Preferred Approach

  • Scouts ahead using Adam to gather intelligence
  • Prefers ranged combat, using longbow from concealed positions
  • Opens combat with Hunter's Mark on priority target
  • Focuses fire on wounded enemies to trigger Colossus Slayer
  • Uses terrain and stealth to gain advantage
  • Saves spell slots for healing or critical Hunter's Mark uses

Round-by-Round Example

Round 1: Bonus action Hunter's Mark, Action attack with longbow (+7, 1d8+3+1d6)

Round 2+: Attack wounded enemies when possible for Colossus Slayer bonus (1d8+3+1d6+1d8 total)

Expertise Areas

Wilderness Survival: Expert tracker and guide in arctic terrain, can navigate without getting lost, finds resources efficiently

Intelligence Gathering: Skilled at observation, uses Adam for reconnaissance, maintains networks of informants

Threat Assessment: Knowledgeable about Red Wizards, ancient magical sites, regional dangers, and political situations

Covert Operations: Experienced in disguise, stealth, and working undercover for extended periods

Notes & Backstory Summary

Role: Senior Harper agent operating in the northern reaches, specializing in intelligence gathering and threat monitoring.

Base of Operations: Fjord's End, which he is developing as a Harper safe haven and operations center.

Signature Method: Uses Adam the raven as an extension of his awareness, often knowing of arrivals and events before physically present.

Recruitment: Responsible for bringing Magnus Irongrip into the Harpers. Actively seeks capable individuals to aid Harper missions.

Current Focus: Monitoring Red Wizard activities, investigating ancient magical sites, and protecting remote settlements from various threats.

Personality: Patient, observant, cautious about revealing information. Speaks in measured tones and always maintains awareness of surroundings. Deeply loyal to Harper ideals and those under his protection.
