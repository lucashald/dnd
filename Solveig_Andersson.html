Solveig Andersson

About Solveig

Solveig Andersson is a kind-hearted older woman known for her delicious pies and breads. She runs a small bakery in the village, where her treats bring joy to everyone who visits.

Family

Solveig has a son, Tormon, who is a skilled hunter. However, Thomas has been missing for a couple of days, and Solveig is growing increasingly worried about his safety.

Personality

Solveig is warm and welcoming, always ready to share a story or a slice of pie. Despite her cheerful demeanor, her recent worries about her son have cast a shadow over her usually bright spirit.
