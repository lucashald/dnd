üåç Regional Context

The Easting Reach fjord cuts through the landscape like a giant's sword, with the ancient Rawlinswood (including the corrupted Dunwood) spreading south and west, while the Giantspire Mountains rise to the east. This is a land where the collapse of Luruar has left a power vacuum, Harper agents work in the shadows, and ancient magics stir in forgotten places.

Key Conflict: Red Wizards of Thay use the magical properties of the Stone Forest to annually renew their binding of the ancient black dragon Putrifax. The Harpers plan Operation Songbreak to disrupt this ritual and free the dragon, hoping her hatred will turn against her captors.
