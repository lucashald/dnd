Rock gnome, female, Deranged Inventor

Current Situation

Facktore sits in the chair of her autoloading crossbow platform in area G7, having become completely obsessed with her invention. When she sees strangers, she decides to "test out the contraption" on them, viewing intruders more as test subjects than threats.

Mental State

  • Utterly consumed by her work
  • Can barely string together coherent sentences
  • Views combat as "product testing"
  • Deranged and dangerous but not malicious
  • Stops fighting when her contraption ceases to function

Enhanced Contraption (Level 3 Version)

For your level 3 party, Facktore's device has been upgraded:

  • Alchemist's Fire Bolts: Normal damage + 1d4 fire damage for 2 rounds
  • Secondary Platform: Manual smaller crossbow she can operate
  • Fire Beetle Component: Contains harvested fire beetle glands for alchemical ammunition

Personality

  • Brilliant but completely unhinged inventor
  • Speaks in technical jargon and half-finished thoughts
  • More interested in mechanical perfection than social interaction
  • Not evil, just dangerously focused

Combat Behavior

  • Uses her contraption to test its capabilities
  • Switches between automated and manual firing
  • Stops fighting if the device is destroyed or she's incapacitated
  • Can potentially be reasoned with using technical language

Potential Outcomes

  • Defeated: Continues working on repairs, ignores party
  • Negotiated With: Might provide technical expertise
  • Left Alone: Remains at her post, tests device on future intruders

Knowledge

  • Understands the layout of Gnomengarde's defenses
  • Knows about various mechanical traps throughout the complex
  • Has technical expertise that could be useful for equipment modification
