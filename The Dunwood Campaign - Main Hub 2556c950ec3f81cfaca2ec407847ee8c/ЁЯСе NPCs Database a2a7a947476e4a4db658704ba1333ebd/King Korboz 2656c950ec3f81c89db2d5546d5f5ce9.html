Rock gnome, male, King of Gnomengarde

Current Situation

King Korboz has lost his mind after being attacked by the mimic and nearly killed. In his madness, he has locked himself and King Gnerkli in their bedroom, believing this is the only way to protect them from the "shapechanger" threat.

The Madness

  • Believes there's a dangerous shapechanger loose in Gnomengarde
  • Distrusts everyone, thinking they might be the mimic in disguise
  • Has imprisoned Gnerkli for "protection"
  • Speaks to visitors through the locked door
  • Won't open the door until convinced the threat is eliminated

Personality (When Sane)

  • Caring ruler who loves his people
  • Protective of his husband Gnerkli
  • Intelligent and reasonable
  • Generous to those who help his community

Recovery

Korboz regains his sanity when:

  • Shown convincing proof the mimic is dead (DC 12 Charisma Persuasion check)
  • The actual mimic's remains are presented to him
  • Multiple witnesses confirm the threat is eliminated

Appearance

  • Wears a jagged metal crown matching Gnerkli's
  • Dressed in a colorful patchwork cloak
  • Carries a flask of solvent that dissolves glue
  • Shows signs of trauma and stress

Rewards

Once his sanity returns, Korboz gives the party a hat of wizardry in gratitude.

Key Knowledge

  • Only NPC who knows the true nature of the threat (mimic)
  • Can identify what happened to the missing gnomes
  • Understands the timeline of the creature's attacks
