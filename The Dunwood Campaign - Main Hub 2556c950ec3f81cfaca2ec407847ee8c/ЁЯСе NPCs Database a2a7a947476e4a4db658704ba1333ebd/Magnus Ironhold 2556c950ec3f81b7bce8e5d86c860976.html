Overview

Magnus Ironhold is a dwarf innkeeper and former adventurer who runs the Glacier's Rest Inn in Fjord's End, a small settlement in the northern reaches of the Giantspire Mountains. His establishment serves as a crucial gathering point for local residents and travelers, while his adventuring background makes him a valuable source of local knowledge and a natural leader during times of crisis.
