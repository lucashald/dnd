"Moonwhisper" (Senior Harper Agent)

Overview

"Moonwhisper" is the operational codename for a senior Harper agent coordinating anti-Thayan operations in northeastern Faerûn, specifically overseeing the mission to disrupt the Red Wizards' control over the ancient black dragon Putrifax. This agent represents the Harper network's deep commitment to preventing Thayan expansion and maintaining regional balance of power.

Personal Details

  • Codename: Moonwhisper
  • True Identity: Classified (likely descendant of Archdruid Selenthe Moonwhisper)
  • Organization: The Harpers
  • Rank: Senior operational coordinator
  • Specialization: Counter-Thayan operations and magical intelligence
  • Base of Operations: Mobile, with connections throughout the former Silver Marches

Operational Background

Moonwhisper coordinates Harper activities across multiple cells and regions, with particular expertise in operations involving magical threats and ancient supernatural dangers. The agent's codename suggests a connection to the original Archdruid Selenthe Moonwhisper, who led the coalition that first bound Putrifax centuries ago, indicating either familial heritage or spiritual succession to that legacy.

Current Mission: Operation "Songbreak"

Moonwhisper serves as the operational coordinator for the Harper mission to disrupt Thayan dragon control:

  • Primary Objective: Break Thayan control over Putrifax during annual binding ceremony
  • Secondary Goals: Gather intelligence on Red Wizard northern operations
  • Method: Coordinated strike during winter solstice ritual to disrupt concentration
  • Authorization: Direct approval from Storm Silverhand and Twilight Hall
