Tharchion Nyth

Overview

Tharchion Nyth is a powerful Red Wizard and the current binder of the ancient black dragon Putrifax, representing one of the most dangerous Thayan operatives in northeastern Faerûn. She discovered the weakened dragon in 1456 DR and has successfully maintained control over the creature for over three decades, making her a key target for Harper operations seeking to disrupt Thayan dragon control schemes.

Discovery & Control of Putrifax

In 1456 DR, the young Red Wizard Nyth led an expedition that discovered references to the "Acid Death imprisoned in living stone" in captured Narfell texts. Her team located the Stone Forest and found the weakened dragon bound by ancient druidic magic. Rather than attempt to free Putrifax, Nyth realized they could exploit the existing binding by layering their own geas spells over the druidic enchantments.

This innovative approach allowed the Thayans to control the dragon while using the limestone's natural properties to make her more manageable, turning the prison into a tool of control.

Annual Ritual Operations

Nyth coordinates the annual dragon binding ceremony each winter solstice, when natural magical forces are at their strongest:

  • Duration: Seven-day ritual requiring constant magical attention
  • Participants: Seven Red Wizards working in coordinated shifts
  • Location: Stone Forest in the Giantspire Mountains
  • Security: 40-60 Thayan soldiers providing perimeter protection
  • Resistance: Dragon's resistance grows stronger each year, requiring 40+ attempts
