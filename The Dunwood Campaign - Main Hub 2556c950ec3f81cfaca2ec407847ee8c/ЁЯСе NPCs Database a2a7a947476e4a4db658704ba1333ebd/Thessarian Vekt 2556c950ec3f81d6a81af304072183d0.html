Overview

Thessarian Vekt is a Red Wizard of Conjuration who participates in the annual ritual to bind Putrifax, the ancient black dragon controlled by Thayan forces. His journal reveals the internal politics and magical challenges surrounding the dragon control operation, making him both a potential source of intelligence and a direct threat to Harper operations seeking to disrupt Thayan activities.
