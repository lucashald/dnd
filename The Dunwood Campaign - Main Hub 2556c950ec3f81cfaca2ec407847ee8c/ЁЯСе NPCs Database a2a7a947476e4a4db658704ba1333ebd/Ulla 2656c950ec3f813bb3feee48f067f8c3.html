Rock gnome, female, Guard

Current Assignment

Ulla is stationed at the guard post (area G9) on a 10-foot high ledge, working alongside Pog to challenge anyone entering Gnomengarde. She's following orders from the kings to watch for "shapechangers."

Guard Protocol

  • Calls out "Who goes there?" in Gnomish when anyone enters
  • Orders are to "attack shapechangers on sight"
  • Since anyone could be a shapechanger, she's suspicious of all visitors
  • Will attack unless visitors can prove they're not shapechangers

Challenge Resolution

Party members can avoid combat with successful checks:

  • DC 10 Charisma (Deception): Trick the gnomes into thinking they have an audience with the kings
  • DC 10 Charisma (Intimidation): Scare them into thinking harm to PCs = Gnomengarde's destruction
  • DC 10 Charisma (Persuasion): Convince them PCs can't be shapechangers (shapechangers would take less conspicuous forms)

Personality

  • Dutiful and follows orders precisely
  • Paranoid about shapechanger infiltration
  • Protective of her community
  • Works well with her partner Pog
  • Nervous but determined to do her job

Key Information

  • Knows about the standing orders regarding shapechangers
  • Understands basic layout of Gnomengarde
  • Can provide directions to other areas if befriended
  • Aware that something has the leadership spooked

Combat Stats

  • Uses standard rock gnome recluse statistics
  • Positioned on elevated ledge for tactical advantage
  • Will flee if seriously threatened
  • Coordinates attacks with Pog
